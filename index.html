Gift Revenue & Exchange Rates
⌄
Gift Revenue Split
  • Hosts receive 100% crystals from regular gifts
  • Hosts receive a 5% share from lucky gifts
Recharge & Exchange
  • Google Store recharge: 1 USD = 42,500 Coins

03
Weekly Room Reward (10%)
⌄

Rewards are paid in coins with no minimum room contribution target. Hosts must meet the on-air requirement to qualify.

  • On-air requirement: 5 days / 10 hours per week, minimum 30 minutes per day to count as 1 valid day
  • Complete 5 valid days + 10 hours → earn a 10% coin reward on the previous week's total gift contribution
  • Distributed every Monday — hosts must broadcast from their own designated room to qualify
🔥 ExampleA room receives 8,000,000 in gift contribution last week (regular gifts at 100% + lucky gifts at 5%). The host completed 7 days / 20 hours on-air, so they receive 8,000,000 × 10% = 800,000 coins as the weekly reward the following Monday.
04
Withdrawal & Settlement
⌄
  • Hosts and agency owners can freely exchange, transfer and withdraw funds at any time
  • The company settles and processes all withdrawal requests every Thursday

🏢 Agency Guidelines

Agency Policy

Agencies recruit and manage hosts, and earn a 12% commission on their hosts' income. The more your hosts earn, the more your agency earns — automatically.

12%
Commission on host earnings
Weekly
Settled with host cycle
01
How the 12% Works
⌄
  • For every dollar a host earns in salary, the managing agency earns 12% on top — paid by the platform, not deducted from the host
  • Commission scales with host level, so growing your hosts directly grows agency income
  • Agency salary is settled on the same cycle as host salary
🔥 ExampleA Level 8 host earns $120.00 host salary. The managing agency earns $120 × 12% = $20.00 in agency commission for that host, in the same period.

Admin & Super Admin

The management program rewards leaders on their monthly crystal income. Build a team of agencies, hit your target and unlock override commissions.

01
Commission Overview
⌄
IdentityBase TargetDirectOverride

02
Admin Eligibility
⌄
  • Base target: reach a minimum monthly crystal income of 25,000,000 Crystals
  • Agencies: maintain and manage at least 10 active agencies
  • Maintenance: hit the minimum crystal target every month to keep the identity and benefits
  • Removal: failing the base target for 2 consecutive months removes the Admin identity
03
Super Admin Promotion
⌄
  • Eligibility: first fulfil all Admin requirements and maintain good standing
  • Crystal target: achieve 500,000,000 base target crystals
  • Approval: meeting the target does not automatically grant the identity — promotion is reviewed and approved by the official Hayat Chat team
Super Admin Benefits
  • 3% commission on eligible crystal income
  • 1% override from Admins
  • Lead and support Admin teams across the region
04
General Rules
⌄
  • A minimum of 10 active agencies is required to obtain and maintain Admin identity
  • Missing the base target for 2 consecutive months results in identity removal
  • Super Admin promotion requires both target achievement and official approval
  • Hayat Chat may review, suspend or revoke identities for fraud, inactivity or policy violations
